Privacy Policy for Quantum Luck 6/49

Last updated: June 26, 2026

Information We Do Not Collect

Quantum Luck 6/49 does not collect, store, or share any personal information or user data.

How the App Works

This app generates lottery number combinations locally on your device. All calculations are performed on your phone - no data is transmitted to our servers or any third parties.

The app contains three Play Sets of number combinations for your use. No login, account, or personal information is required.

Permissions

This app requires no special permissions. It does not access your contacts, location, camera, microphone, or storage.

Third-Party Services

This app does not use analytics, advertising, or any third-party SDKs that collect data.
